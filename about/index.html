About

Posted at — Dec 30, 2019

Anoop is a SharePoint and Office 365 Developer at Content and Code Ltd based in London, UK. He has worked across the entire lifecycle of projects, from gathering and analyzing requirements to handing over the solution to managed services. He has 7+ years of experience in SharePoint development and has worked mainly on Office 365, SharePoint Online, SharePoint 2013 and 2010.

Anoop is a regular contributor to the Office 365 Developer Patterns and Practices project on GitHub. He also writes blog articles dedicated to his experience with SharePoint development.
